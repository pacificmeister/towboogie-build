💡 Community Build Tips

  • Mount motor at the very rear — front/center causes nosedive
  • Tow point goes under the hull, not on top
  • Add 5° downward pitch to motor pod
  • Conformal coat all PCBs before assembly
  • Aluminum profile (25×25mm) = game-changer for rigidity
  • Always bench-test electronics before hull install
